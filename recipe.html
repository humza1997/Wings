Recipe

Oh so you want to make some wings now? Heres a list of ingredients you'll need:

  • Raw Chicken Wings
  • Flour
  • Cayene pepper
  • Paprika
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oil

    Follow these steps for the perfect chicken wings:

  1. Combine all dry ingredients to make your dry rub
  2. Coat the raw chicken wings in the dry rub
  3. Heat up your oil to 150deg C
  4. Slowly drop your wings into the oil or lower into using a basket
  5. Cook for a minimum of 7 minutes, increasing the time by 1 minute for every 10 wings added.
  6. Take out the wings when they have a crispy exterior
